Travel Information

Many of you come and leave at different times. If you come by car and have some seats left, I'd just ask you to specify this in the table below. For those who come and go by train from Berlin let me give you some hopefully useful info.

Coming on Friday or Saturday:

The most convenient travel is to take the RE5 from Gesundbrunnen to Gransee. The travel just takes 45 minutes. The train rolls about every hour for example:
   Friday: 16:55, 17:55, 18:55 o'clock
   Saturday:8:55, 9:55, 10:55 o'clock

If you are at the Gransee train station, you have different options

  • Call me or Anna and I pick you up, it's just 10 minuets away by car.
  • Bring your bike and cycle the last meters. It's about 6 km.
  • Take the bus to Buberow Kirche. For example at 18:01 o'clock take the R840 and arrive at 18:09.
    I recommend to use vbb.de to check the busses. Looks more convenient then Google maps.

Leaving Saturday or Sunday:

Again, it's easiest to take the RE5 from Gransee to Gesundbrunnen. On both days the train is leaving Gransee every hour even until quite late in the night, for those who leave on Saturday but wants to enjoy the BBQ. For example

   Saturday: 12:26, 13:23, or 20:26, 21:23 o'clock. The last train to Berlin leaves Gransee at 23:23 o'clock.
   Sunday:11:23, 12:26 o'clock

To get to Grannsee you have again the same option as above

  • Ask me, Anna or some other with a car.
  • Take the bike.
  • Take the bus from Buberow Kirche. Check the schedule at vbb.de
